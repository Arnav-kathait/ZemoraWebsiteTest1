About Us

We are a student led organisation that works to promoting recycling of E-wastes in our economy . With the rise in technology on the contrary there's a huge rise to electric wastes, But what do we do? We act as a collection group we host collection drives and transport all the E wastes we collect to certified recyclers so they can recycle them and make new products, which not only saves our environment form damage and pollution but help make new devices without loosing our resources.

Our Services

  • Act as a Bridge for people wanting to donate e-waste for a better cause.
